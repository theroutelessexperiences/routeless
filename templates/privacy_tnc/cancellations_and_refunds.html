Cancellation & Refund Policy

Clear, transparent, and fair — so you can book with confidence.

All bookings made through The Routeless are governed by a standardised cancellation and refund policy determined solely by Routeless, in compliance with applicable Indian laws.

Refund Eligibility

Refund eligibility depends on:

  • Timing of cancellation
  • Safety considerations
  • Weather conditions
  • Regulatory or operational constraints

Third-party operators listed on Routeless do not have the authority to approve or deny refunds.

User Rights & Transparency

Users will always be informed of:

  • Applicable cancellation terms at the time of booking
  • Any non-refundable components (permits, insurance, fees)

Routeless ensures that all policies are clear, transparent, and free from misleading practices.

Platform Authority

Routeless reserves the exclusive right to:

  • Approve, deny, or modify refund requests
  • Offer full or partial refunds
  • Provide rescheduling or alternative experiences

All decisions made by Routeless are final and binding, subject to applicable law.

Mandatory Refund Scenarios

If an experience cannot be delivered due to:

  • Government restrictions
  • Safety risks
  • Weather or environmental disruptions
  • Operational failure by Routeless or partners

Users will be offered:

  • A full or proportionate refund
  • Or a rescheduling option where feasible

Non-Refundable Components

Certain components may not be refundable, including:

  • Government permits
  • Insurance fees
  • Third-party booking charges

These will always be clearly disclosed at the time of booking.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days, depending on banking and payment gateway timelines.

Dispute Resolution

In case of disputes:

  • Users should first contact Routeless customer support
  • All matters are governed by applicable Indian laws

Users retain the right to approach consumer forums under the Consumer Protection Act, 2019.
